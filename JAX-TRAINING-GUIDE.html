01 What This Tool Does

The COAI Lead Engine is a prospecting machine that scans Google Maps for local businesses in Bakersfield and surrounding areas, then scores each one based on how badly they need your services. It pulls real data — phone numbers, websites, star ratings, review counts — and ranks every business by "opportunity score." The higher the score, the weaker their digital presence, and the easier the close.

◉
The Core Logic: A business with no website, 2 stars, and 4 reviews is in pain. They're losing leads to competitors every day. You're calling them with a $1,200 solution that fixes all of it. That's why we score for weakness, not strength.

03 Picking Your Hunt (Location + Industries)
📍
Section 1 · Location & Radius
The City / Area field defaults to Bakersfield, CA. You can change it to any city.

Radius options:
• 2 miles — Core 661, downtown Bakersfield only
• 5 miles — Greater Bakersfield (recommended starting point)
• 10 miles — All of Kern County
• 25–50 miles — Central Valley / max range
🏭
Section 2 · Industries
Pick which business types to scan. You have three shortcuts at the top:

• Select All — scans every category (slow but thorough)
• Clear All — resets everything
• Hot Only — pre-selects the highest-converting categories: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, auto repair, dentist, hair salon, locksmith, AI consulting, and more

You can also click individual categories or use the All button next to each vertical (Trades, Auto, Medical, etc.) to toggle that whole group.
💡
Pro Tip: Start with Hot Only + 5-mile radius. You'll get the best leads fastest. Only go wider if you're running dry on a category.

06 How the Scoring System Works

Each lead gets a score from 0–100 based on their pain signals. The higher the score, the more they need you.

Signal Default Points Why It Matters
No Website +40 They're invisible online. You're selling them visibility. Easiest pitch.
Low Rating (under 3.5★) +25 Reputation damage is costing them leads daily. They know it hurts.
No Rating +20 New or inactive business. Needs reputation foundation.
Low Reviews (under 10) +15 They're not capturing happy customers. Easy system to pitch.
No Phone Listed +10 Losing calls before they happen. Basic setup problem.
Zero Reviews +10 Combined with no website = 50+ score by default. HOT lead.
⚙
Score Weights are editable. In Section 3 of the sidebar, scroll down to see the weight sliders. Jason may adjust these for different campaigns.

Click the CRM tab at the top of the main panel to switch to the CRM view. This shows all your leads in a table format — easier for tracking.

Use the Search box to find any lead by name, address, phone, or email. Use the Category and Status dropdowns to narrow by industry or contact stage.

Every lead has one of three statuses:

Contacted — You've reached out. Follow up in 3–5 days.

Auto-Save: The CRM saves automatically to your browser every time you make a change. You don't need to hit a save button. But do NOT clear your browser cache without first doing a JSON backup (Section 10).

09 Exporting Data

At the bottom of the Prospector panel you'll see the export bar.

↓ Export CSV
Exports ALL leads with full data: name, category, address, phone, enriched phone, email, website, rating, reviews, score, status, notes, and scan date. Opens in Excel or Google Sheets.
↓ Hot Only CSV
Exports only leads with score 50+. Smaller file, just the top-tier targets. Best for sharing with Jason.
✕ Clear All
Warning: This clears all leads from the view. Make sure to export first or do a JSON backup before clearing.
10 Backup & Restore

CRM data lives in your browser. To protect it, use the backup buttons in the sidebar under Session Stats.

↓
↓ Backup JSON
Downloads a .json file with all your leads, statuses, notes, and timestamps. Save this to a folder or Google Drive. Do this at least weekly — daily if you're scanning every day.
↑
↑ Restore JSON
Loads a backup file back into the tool. You'll be asked to confirm before it replaces your current CRM. Use this if you switch computers or accidentally clear the browser.
🚨
Critical: Clearing your browser cookies/cache or using a private/incognito window will wipe the CRM. Always back up before switching devices or clearing browser data.
11 Daily Workflow — The Repeatable Play
1
Open the tool, enter your key
Takes 10 seconds. Key stays saved for the session.
2
Click "Hot Only" → pick a vertical → Scan
Focus on one vertical per session (e.g. just Trades, or just Medical). Keeps your calls focused and consistent.
3
Filter to "🔥 Hot" leads only
Click the 🔥 Hot filter chip. Work top-to-bottom by score. Don't skip HOT leads to chase ones you "like better."
4
For each HOT lead: Verify → Script → Contact
1. Click Maps to confirm the business is real and active. 2. Click Outreach → hit ⚡ AI Personalize. 3. Call, DM, or email using the script. 4. Hit Contacted immediately after.
5
End of session: Backup + Export
Click ↓ Backup JSON to save your CRM state. Click ↓ Export CSV for a snapshot. Log your stats with Jason.
🎯
Target KPIs: 20–30 HOT leads contacted per day. 3–5 callbacks. 1–2 audit calls booked. 1 close per week to start. These numbers go up as you get comfortable with the scripts.
12 Troubleshooting
Problem Fix
ACCESS DENIED Wrong App Access Key. Double check with Jason — it's case-sensitive.
Zero results after scan Expand radius to 25–50 miles. Select more categories. Wait 60 seconds and retry.
Leads show "No Phone" but they definitely have one Make sure "Skip Google Place Details" is unchecked in Section 3. Place Details is what pulls accurate phones and websites.
⚡ AI Personalize just shows the same template Run a scan first — this sets your session key. If you're on a fresh tab, AI can't authenticate.
Stale badge on old leads Lead was scanned 30+ days ago. Re-scan that category to get fresh data before calling.
CRM is empty after reopening Browser cleared its cache. Always restore from your most recent JSON backup using ↑ Restore JSON.
Tool is slow during enrichment Normal — the tool is fetching Place Details for each listing. 40 leads takes about 60–90 seconds. Don't close the tab.
